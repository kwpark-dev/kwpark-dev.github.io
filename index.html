Robo sapiens Project

The Robo sapiens Project aims to develop a morphology-agnostic RFM (Robot Foundation Model) that generalizes across robotic embodiments and sensory systems through RL (Reinforcement Learning), PINN (Physics Informed Neural Network), Vision, and SF (Sensor Fusion).
