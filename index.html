Welcome to Robo sapiens Project!

Overview

The Robo sapiens Project aims to develop a morphology-agnostic RFM (Robot Foundation Model) that generalizes across robotic embodiments and sensory systems through RL (Reinforcement Learning), PINN (Physics Informed Neural Network), Vision, and SF (Sensor Fusion). Research milestones are planned as follow.

Phase 1: Applied RL Experiments

Phase 2: RFM Foundations & RL Integration

Phase 3: Robot Application
